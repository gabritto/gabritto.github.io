Personal

  • Origin

    I was born in Brazil, in a coastal city called Recife. It is very hot and humid all year, with a slightly less hot rainy season.
    It is one of the main capitals of Brazil's northeast region. If you ever visit, make sure you eat bolo de rolo, and take a tour through Recife Antigo.

  • Personal Values

    Brazil is not the easiest country to live in, and so being born and raised here, I had to learn to be resilient (and a little bit pessimistic). I also learned that I strongly believed in being kind to others, and helping others, and to me this is an act of resistance.

    I believe in collaboration, and that we should strive to build inclusive and accepting communities. I aim to be able to give back to the communities I am part of, even though I'm often not sure of how to do this.

  • Pets

    I love having pets, and I have had many of them in my life. I have had mice, hamsters, guinea pigs, and a rabbit. I have also had many dogs. My first two dogs have sadly passed away, and I will always miss them.

    In my Canadian home (where I currently live), I have two terribly cute guinea pigs.
    In my Brazilian home, I have two tortoises and two dogs: a very sweet german shepherd and a loving rescue mutt.
    I also like cats, but never had the chance to have one.

  • Hobbies

    Besides dogs and pets, I enjoy reading books (both fiction and non-fiction), watching shows, doing arts and crafts, and eating chocolate.
